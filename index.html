April 27 – May 3

Penn [Dis]Connects is back this year and better than ever! We’re planning a full week of programing to challenge our relationship with technology and connect us with people. To stay updated on events throughout the week, sign up for our listserv below.

Monday

Dinner + Discussion with Justin McDaniel

Join the teacher of the Monk Course in exploring how technology affects our relationship with the world, others, and ourselves.

Campout + Outdoor Movie Night

After dinner, come outside for an outdoor screening of "Her" on High Rise field and roast s’mores in a bonfire under the stars.

Wednesday

[Dis]Connected BYOs

Go to a BYO with some friends, turn your phones off for the duration, and we'll reimburse $20 of your bill!

Thursday

Bike Ride to Manayunk

Join us for a ride beautiful disconnected ride through Fairmount Park to Manayunk, where we will stop for dinner.

Friday

First Friday

Come with us to explore Philadelphia's galleries for free art and drinks, SEPTA tokens on us.

Saturday

[Dis]Connected Picnic

Join us as we finish our week with a bang. BYO food and we’ll provide the rest — picnic blankets, live music, and a good time.

You can also stay up-to-date by liking our Facebook page or following us on Twitter.
